How ICO Works

  • platform

    PLATFORM Solar DAO creates a decentralized platform that allows everyone to easily participate in PV solar plants construction across the globe. Helps to subsidize Solar Projects in one level with large investors with no extra fees on 3rd parties.

  • interface

    Solar DAO creates Dashboard, the one single interface. It provides all details about current and future project. Helps to monitor all PV plants in Solar DAO as snippets, see how the work is going in projects under construction.

  • community

    Solar DAO helps to reduce risks, costs and the major part of barriers when funding PV solar plants and other RES projects all over the world. Prevents spendings on low effective technical solutions and overpriced equipment when funding.
